DMS Integration Matrix
Reynolds Push-Based
  • Customers

    Customer updates push automatically as part of standard workflows.

  • Vehicles

    Vehicle information is included with RO and customer pushes.

  • Appointments

    Pushes from myKaarma when generated or updated (if any appointments are scheduled).

  • ROs

    ROs push from ServiceCart, Check-In, or configured workflow triggers.

  • Parts / Invoices

    Can push automatically when workflows support parts invoice generation.

  • History

    History syncs via RO traffic and labor codes.

Automate Push-Based
  • Customers

    Customers push automatically into Automate.

  • Vehicles

    Vehicle updates flow with RO traffic.

  • Appointments

    Limited appointment update support; details may vary by store.

  • ROs

    No RO write-back path from myKaarma.

  • Parts

    No parts invoice push supported.

  • History

    Primarily presentation only with limited push traffic.

CDK Pull-Based
  • Customers

    Pull every 15 minutes; historical lookup available on-demand.

  • Vehicles

    Vehicle info comes with RO records.

  • Appointments

    Pull every 15 minutes; myKaarma can request additional refreshes.

  • ROs

    Pull every 5 minutes; write-back is on-demand.

  • Parts & Pricing

    Invoices pull every 15 minutes; on-demand pricing available in ServiceCart.

  • History

    Nightly sync of RO and F&I history (≈2 years).

Dealertrack / Arkona Pull-Based
  • Customers

    Pull every 5 minutes; historical requests supported.

  • Vehicles

    Vehicle lookup on-demand; also comes with RO data.

  • Appointments

    Pull every 15 minutes.

  • ROs

    Pull every 2 minutes; write-back is on-demand.

  • Parts & Pricing

    Parts invoices pull every 15 minutes.

  • History

    Nightly sync for RO and customer history.

Lightyear / Dealerbuilt Pull-Based
  • Customers

    Pull every 5 minutes.

  • Vehicles

    Loaded with RO activity.

  • Appointments

    Pull every 5 minutes.

  • ROs

    Pull every 2 minutes; on-demand write-back.

  • Parts

    Parts invoices pull every 5 minutes (varies by store).

  • History

    Nightly RO history sync; on-demand refresh supported.

PBS Pull-Based
  • Customers

    Customer pulls limited; relies heavily on RO activity.

  • Vehicles

    Vehicle sync primarily RO-related.

  • Appointments

    Pull every 5 minutes; some push varies by workflow.

  • ROs

    Pull every 2 minutes; write-back is on-demand.

  • Parts

    Parts invoice pulling is not universal.

  • History

    Nightly RO history sync.

Tekion Pull-Based
  • Customers

    Customers arrive with the RO; no independent customer pull.

  • Vehicles

    Vehicle flow attached to RO updates.

  • Appointments

    Pull every 5 minutes; some write-back planned.

  • ROs

    Pull every 5 minutes; write-back enabled per store.

  • Parts & Pricing

    Planned and supported in roadmap.

  • History

    Historical RO load available during deployment.

DMS Integration

Reference how myKaarma pushes to and pulls from each DMS. This is a read-only resource for trainers.

DMS Integration – Summary by DMS

Automatic / Real-time
On Demand / Button Driven
With RO Only
Planned / Future
Not Available / N/A
CDK Pull-Based
  • Customer / Vehicle Auto

    Customer and vehicle info refreshes automatically on a regular cadence (about every 15 minutes).

  • Appointments On Demand

    Appointment data is pulled as needed and when appointments are scheduled.

  • RO History & Opcodes Auto

    RO updates and opcode details sync automatically, with nightly historical RO pulls (about 2 years).

Reynolds Push-Based
  • Customer / Vehicle Auto

    Customer and vehicle updates are pushed automatically from myKaarma into the DMS.

  • Appointments & ROs On Demand

    Appointments and RO activity update on demand when events are scheduled or changes occur.

  • Historical & Special Data Auto

    Historical customer and RO information is kept current via automatic writebacks where supported.

Dealertrack / Arkona Pull-Based
  • Customer / Vehicle Auto

    Customer and vehicle info refreshes automatically every few minutes.

  • Appointments On Demand

    Appointments are pulled on demand when scheduled and during active use.

  • RO & Historical Auto

    RO updates and historical RO data sync regularly with nightly jobs for history.

PBS Pull-Based
  • Customer / Vehicle Auto

    Customer and vehicle info is pulled as needed rather than pushed back.

  • Appointments & ROs On Demand

    Appointments and open RO information are pulled on demand and on scheduled intervals.

  • Parts / Pricing On Demand

    Parts and pricing information is pulled from PBS when required in ServiceCart.

Tekion Pull-Based
  • Customer / Vehicle With RO

    Customer and vehicle details are pulled with the RO and via appointment context.

  • Appointments / ROs Planned

    Some appointment and RO data flows occur via planned integrations and scheduled jobs.

  • Historical & Opcodes Planned

    Historical RO and opcode detail is primarily available through planned reporting integrations.

Automate Push-Based
  • Customer / Vehicle Auto

    myKaarma pushes customer and vehicle updates automatically into Automate.

  • RO Creation With RO

    ROs created or updated in myKaarma are pushed into Automate during check-in workflows.

  • Historical & Special Data N/A

    Most of the historical and special-order data is maintained on the DMS side; myKaarma focuses on live RO pushes.

Lightyear / DealerBuilt Pull-Based
  • Customer / Vehicle Auto

    Customer and vehicle records are pulled every few minutes from the DMS.

  • Appointments / ROs On Demand

    Appointments and RO details are pulled as needed and via scheduled syncs.

  • Historical Data Auto

    Historical RO and customer data is synced nightly, with on-demand requests available from myKaarma.
